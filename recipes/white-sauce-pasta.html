White Sauce Pasta

White sauce pasta is a creamy and delicious Italian dish made with pasta and a rich, velvety white sauce. The sauce, known as Béchamel, is typically made from butter, flour, and milk, creating a smooth and flavorful base. It is often seasoned with salt, pepper, and nutmeg, and can be enhanced with ingredients like garlic, cheese, or herbs. The pasta, usually varieties like penne, fettuccine, or spaghetti, is cooked until al dente and then tossed in the white sauce. Vegetables, chicken, or seafood can be added for extra flavor and texture. White sauce pasta is a comforting and indulgent meal enjoyed by many around the world.

  • Pasta: 8 ounces (penne, fettuccine, or your choice)
  • Butter: 2 tablespoons
  • All-Purpose Flour: 2 tablespoons
  • Milk: 2 cups
  • Salt,Nutmeg and Black Pepper: To taste
  • Garlic: 1-2 cloves
  • Grated Cheese: 1/2 cup
  • Vegetables or Protein:

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta: Fill a large pot with water, add a pinch of salt, and bring it to a boil. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  2. Prepare the White Sauce: In a large pan , melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic to the melted butter and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Make the Roux: Add 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour to the butter (and garlic) mixture. Stir continuously with a whisk or wooden spoon for about 1-2 minutes until the flour is well incorporated and slightly golden, forming a roux.
  4. Add Milk: Gradually add 2 cups of milk to the roux, stirring constantly to avoid lumps. Continue to stir until the mixture thickens and becomes a smooth, creamy sauce.
  5. Season the Sauce: Add salt and black pepper to taste. Add a pinch of nutmeg if desired. If using cheese, stir in 1/2 cup of grated cheese until melted and well combined.
  6. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the white sauce in the pan. Stir gently to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce. Saute the vegtables and add them.
