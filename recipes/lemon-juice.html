Lemon Juice

Lemon juice is a tangy, refreshing liquid extracted from the fruit of the lemon tree. It has a sharp, acidic flavor and is rich in vitamin C, making it a popular ingredient in both culinary and beverage preparations. Lemon juice is commonly used to enhance the taste of dishes, from salads and marinades to desserts and beverages. It is also used for its preservative qualities and its ability to add a bright, zesty flavor to recipes. Additionally, lemon juice is a popular natural remedy and is often used in home cleaning solutions due to its antibacterial properties.
